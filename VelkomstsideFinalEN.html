Welcome to Mon Oncle!

In April 2022, we could finally welcome master chef Esben Holmboe Bang's latest invention here in Oslo. We are proud to be able to take you on a traditional French and classic culinary journey with exciting new twists. For groups larger than 8 people, please contact us at booking@mononcle.no
Esben Holmboe Bang is a Danish chef, co-owner and head chef of a number of restaurants in Oslo such as Maaemo, Kafeteria August, the Vandelay with more.
Bang and his crew excelled early, receiving their first Michelin Star already in 2012.
Just four years later in 2016, Bang and Maeemo received three Michelin stars!
