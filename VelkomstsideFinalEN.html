    Welcome to Mon Oncle!

    In April 2022, we could finally welcome master chef Esben Holmboe Bang's latest invention here in Oslo. We are proud to be able to take you on a traditional French and classic culinary journey with exciting new twists. For groups larger than 8 people, please contact us at booking@mononcle.no


    "The country's best French restaurant" - Godt, 2022

    "Fantastic lot of good food in stylish premises" - Aftenposten, 2022

    "Mon Oncle is so classic that it feels completely new" - DN, 2022

    Esben Holmboe Bang is a Danish chef, co-owner and head chef of a number of restaurants in Oslo such asMaaemo, Kafeteria August, the Vandelay and more.


    Bang and his crew excelled early, receiving their firstMichelin star already in 2012.

    Just four years later in 2016, Bang and Maeemo received three Michelin stars!
