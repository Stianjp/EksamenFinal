Fake News

Fake news is a modern tool that can help influence a group of people to a large extent. But how effective is this tool really? Does it only work on older people who read news on their computer? Do people really think critically anymore or do they blindly believe what they read, see and hear? These are some of the questions we thought we would visualize for you with a small round of interviews with a few different people who had no idea what we were doing.
